YOGA APERTA FIRENZE

Guided Practice

Salve e namaste! At Yoga Aperta, we specialize in Vinyasa yoga, a particularly energetic form of yoga linking breath and movement to encourage meditation in motion. We shift quickly through a wide variety of poses, so come prepared to work up a sweat while you center your psyche! Each of our instructors has a unique approach to their practice, and we offer classes for all levels of yoga familiarity. Whether you're a complete beginner or a true yogi, we welcome you to come grow with us.

Mindful Meditation

Life can be stressful, but it never has to be. If you're looking to destress without the workout, we also practice sitting meditation on a daily basis. From 05:00 to 06:00 we hold an open-door silent zazen session, and we invite you to stop by and sit with us for as long as your schedule allows. In the afternoon and evening we offer multiple guided meditations. These sessions are more structured than our morning ones, so plan to stay for the duration.
