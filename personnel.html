Ayo

Prana Flow

Zazen Meditation

Vegan Cooking

Ayo was born in Asheville, a beautiful city in the mountains of North Carolina. Raised with a vegan diet and a yoga-centric lifestyle, he says he was born to be an instructor at Yoga Aperta. A student of art history, Ayo spent a semester abroad in Firenze and knew he had found his true home. His love of art and its history has blossomed into a passion for painting, and his work has been featured in exhibitions across Italy and in the United States.

Don

Although he was born in Wisconsin at Fort McCoy, Don was raised in Vicenza at Caserma Ederle Army Base from an early age. Despite his military status, Don says his father had a heart made of granola, and the two practiced yoga together on a regular basis. At eighteen, Don travelled to nearby Venezia and began working as a yoga instructor. During a short trip to Firenze, Don visited our studio for a session. “Between the magnificence of the city,” he says, “and the energy of the studio, I knew what I had to do.” Shortly thereafter he joined our crew.

Although she was born and raised in Firenze, Elaina claims not to count as an Italian instructor. Her father is a painter, her mother a designer, and both are originally from Tennessee, leading Elaina to call herself the world’s only artsy redneck Italian. In truth, she has lived in Firenze all her life, and she has no plans of leaving. Like Ayo, Elaina has a background in art history, but she counts herself a visual artist, working primarily in ceramics and glass.

OUR URBAN OASIS

Ellie

Our farthest-flung instructor, Ellie hails from San Francisco, California. While studying archaeology, she took a semester abroad in Roma, and after multiple trips to Firenze she knew she would one day have to return to the Birthplace of the Renaissance. Ellie’s yoga education began in Roma, where her roommate suggested she visit a studio in order to relieve stress. She never looked back! Archaeology will forever be her first love, but Vinyasa is her passion now.

Ilaria

Indie Vinyasa

Self-Care

Ilaria was born and raised in the commune Libereria Amiatautogestita, located near Monte Amiata in southern Toscana. Daily informal yoga sessions at sunrise informed her desire to be an instructor herself, but she also learned much about sustainable development and gardening in her time in the commune. When she was ready to venture out, Ilaria wandered for several years, finally settling just outside Firenze in Dicomano. Ilaria ventured into town with a friend to visit Yoga Aperta and joined us as an instructor the next day.

Ilaria

Meredith

Meredith was born into a family band in Decatur, Georgia. Called Sojourners Wombley, they toured the United States regularly, and at age four Meredith joined her family on one such tour with mandolin in hand. An immediate fixture, she played with the Sojourners throughout her early life. When Meredith was twenty, her father arranged a tour in Europe, including a concert in Firenze. Immediately taken with the city, Meredith finished the tour, returned to Firenze, and never left. Although she had never practiced yoga, a friend convinced her to take a class at Yoga Aperta, and she quickly became our most devout student. Now Meredith is both our newest instructor and our staff mandolinist.
